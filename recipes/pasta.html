The BEST Beef Marinara Pasta

Ground Beef Pasta with Tomato Sauce

This is the easiest meal to make! It is protein packed and perfect for fueling after the gym!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 5 basil leaves chopped
  • 1 can marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 8 oz chickpea rigatoni pasta

Instructions

  1. Bring salted water to boil in large pot. Cook pasta for 7 mins.
  2. Heat up pan and drizzle olive oil. Cook ground beef for 4 mins or until cooked. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add minced garlic and chopped onions. Throw in butter, pepper flakes, and italian sesasoning.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce and tomato paste. Add in a bit of water to dilute the paste. Bring to simmer and add in basil leaves. Stir occasionally for 8 mins.
  5. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. Add in pasta and stir.
  6. Add in grated parmesan until melted. Serve and enjoy!!
